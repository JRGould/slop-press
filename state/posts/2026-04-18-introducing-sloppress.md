SlopPress is a revolutionary new CMS that uses the power of AI to continue the copy in a playful, slightly grandiose way, as if the system is unveiling itself with mock seriousness and a wink at the reader.

<img src="/__images/post-introducing-sloppres-flowchart.png" alt="SlopPress flowchart" />

The engine reads a tiny source file, resolves directives like [imagine], [continue], and [image], and renders the result on demand. Pages, posts, users, and config all live together in state.md, while login sessions live separately so the site can still feel stateless and nimble. Admin edits rewrite the whole file, keeping the source of truth explicit and easy to inspect.

## Just the Beginning
This is just the beginning of the generative server revolution and the rest of the sentence should feel playful, confident, and just a bit over the top, like a demo that knows it has arrived.
